Three restaurant collaborations you don’t want to miss

Charlotte Blair

9 March 2020

Starting from 17 August, Merah Putih Restaurant is taking over Velada at The Westin Resort Nusa Dua, Bali for the next three months. Whisk away your family and friends to experience the rich heritage of Indonesia regional fare in a curated a la carte menu at the family-friendly restaurant. Taste nosh such as Kerang Lengkuas (Rp95,000), Bebek Goreng with palm hearts and colo colo sambal (Rp95,000), Mie Aceh (Rp120,000), West Sumatra’s Domba Kalio (Rp190,000), and many more. It is a wonderful way to taste Indonesia in one seating. Prolong the evening by joining Velada’s trivia night or play a game of pool. Open Wednesday to Sunday, from 5.30pm to 10.30pm.

Kawasan Pariwisata Nusa Dua, BTDC Lot N-3, Nusa Dua. Tel: +62 361 771906
